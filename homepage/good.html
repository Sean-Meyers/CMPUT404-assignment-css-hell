A Little History

I have always been drawn to the world of game development. In fact, it was my passion for game development that initially led me to explore the world of programming. As I delved deeper into the world of computing, however, I discovered a new passion: the art and science of programming itself.

For me, programming is more than just a means to an end; it is a passion in and of itself. I love the feeling of bringing my creations to life, and I find great satisfaction in learning new languages, frameworks, and tools. Although my passion for game development remains strong, I have also developed a keen curiosity in other areas of computing, including Web Development and Machine Learning. I have taken a wide range of courses to explore these areas, and I am always seeking out new challenges and opportunities to learn.

Currently, my specialties include graphics programming, web development, game AI, and Android development. However, I recognize that there is always more to learn, both within and beyond these domains. That is why, despite being a fourth year student, I am committed to continuing my education and pursuing new opportunities for growth.

Recently, I was accepted into the BSc with Specialization, Computing Science with Business Minor program, which has given me the opportunity to explore a new area of interest. While my ultimate goal is to start and run my own successful game development studio, I am keeping my options open and remaining open to new possibilities. With a strong foundation in computing science and a passion for learning and growth, I am excited to see where my journey will take me next.

Who am I!?

Hi there! I'm a fourth-year Computing Science major at the University of Alberta with a minor in Business. I was born and raised in Edmonton, Alberta, and I'm passionate about programming, game design, computers, and the arts!

When I'm not studying or working on programming projects, I love to explore my creative side through sound design, music composition and illustration. I also enjoy reading manga and light novels, watching anime, and playing games in my free time.

I have always had a thing for making games. When I was in elementary school, making games was my go-to way to avoid doing schoolwork. Now that I am in university, making games sometimes IS my schoolwork. I have experience working with Unity and Unreal Engine, and I have taken courses in Game AI, Game Programming, Graphics Programming, GPU Programming, Numerical Methods, Game Design, Software Engineering, Music Composition, and Music Technology.

In addition to my interest in game development, I'm also fascinated by other areas of computing science, particularly Web Development and Machine Learning. I'm always seeking out new challenges and opportunities to learn and grow. I'm grateful for the opportunities I've had so far to pursue my passions and develop my skills.
